How can I get a copy of my payment receipt?

To view your billing history or print a receipt:

1. Once you are logged in, hover the cursor over your name at the top right of any page to see the account menu
2. Select Account > Billing
3. Scroll down and select Payment History and Receipts. Your account billing history will be displayed
4. Select View Receipt for the payment you wish to view or print

 

How can I change my user name?

If you wish to change your user name or password:

1. Once you are logged in, hover the cursor over your name at the top right of any page to see the account menu
2. Select My Profile
3. Select Edit next to the item you wish to change
4. Enter your new information
5. Save your changes

 

Can I cancel at any time or am I committed to a certain period?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. You will have access to the library until the end of your billing cycle, at which time your membership will not be renewed. Members who live in a European Union country may cancel for any reason within 14 days, by sending a written notice to Customer Service. When you're ready to reactivate your membership, go to our website and select the Reactivate link in the upper right corner.

 

How do I update my credit card and billing information?

1. Once you are logged in, hover the cursor over your name at the top right of any page to see the account menu
2. Select Account > Billing
3. In the Billing Information section, select Edit
4. Enter your new information
5. Save your changes

How do I get an activation key?

If you have not received an activation key from us or from the trial subscription provider, contact your provider.
